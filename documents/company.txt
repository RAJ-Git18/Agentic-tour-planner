1. Company Overview

Wunderlust Expeditions Inc. is a global travel and tour management company founded in 2010 with the mission to redefine the art of exploration. Headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland, with regional offices in Tokyo, New York, Cape Town, and Buenos Aires, the company serves over 500,000 travelers annually.

Originally established as a boutique alpine travel agency, Wunderlust Expeditions has evolved into a full-service travel organization providing luxury, adventure, corporate, educational, and wellness travel solutions worldwide.

Legal Structure: Private Corporation
Head Office: Zurich, Switzerland
Operational Regions: Europe, Asia-Pacific, North America, South America, Africa
Industry: Global Travel & Tourism Services

4. Core Services

4.1 Custom Luxury Itineraries
Bespoke travel planning including private aviation, luxury accommodations, curated dining, cultural immersion programs, VIP access experiences, and concierge services.

4.2 Adventure & Expedition Travel
Organized expeditions including high-altitude trekking, polar exploration, marine diving, wildlife safaris, and extreme climate travel. All expeditions follow international safety standards and certified guide protocols.

4.3 Corporate & MICE Travel
End-to-end management of business travel including conferences, incentive trips, exhibitions, executive retreats, logistics coordination, expense tracking systems, and travel risk management.

4.4 Cultural & Educational Tours
Structured educational programs in partnership with universities, museums, and research institutions. Includes academic-led tours, archaeological site visits, heritage programs, and study abroad support.

4.5 Wellness & Retreat Programs
Holistic wellness experiences including yoga retreats, meditation programs, spa therapies, ayurvedic healing, digital detox programs, and mental health–focused travel experiences.

4.6 Digital Travel Services
AI-assisted itinerary planning, mobile concierge support, virtual destination previews, and 24/7 digital communication tools.
